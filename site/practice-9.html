Practice 9

Mock Meet

Warm-Up (10 min)

  • 2 laps easy jog (800m)
  • Dynamic stretches: full routine (A-skips, B-skips, high knees, lunges)
  • 2 x 60m build-ups
  • Brief review of meet-day procedures

Main Workout — Simulated Meet (40 min)

  • Event 1 — 100m Dash: Heats of 4-6 runners, timed results
  • Event 2 — Long Jump: 3 attempts each, measured and ranked
  • Event 3 — 400m Run: Staggered start, full race
  • Event 4 — Shot Put: 3 attempts each, measured and ranked
  • Event 5 — 800m Run: Waterfall start, timed race
  • Event 6 — 4x100m Relay: Teams of 4, race format
  • Athletes rotate through events they plan to compete in
  • Record all results to compare with Practice 1 baselines

Cool-Down (10 min)

  • 1 lap victory walk
  • Light stretching
  • Results review — celebrate improvements from baseline
  • Preview meet-day logistics for the real competition
