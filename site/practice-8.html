Practice 8

Race Strategy

Warm-Up (10 min)

  • 2 laps easy jog (800m)
  • Dynamic stretches: leg swings, walking lunges, A-skips
  • 2 x 100m strides at 75% effort

Main Workout (40 min)

  • Sprint strategy (15 min):
  • Lane positioning — staggered starts, running the curve
  • Practice 200m with proper curve technique — lean into the turn
  • Finish line lean — practice dipping at the line
  • Distance strategy (15 min):
  • Tactical positioning — when to lead vs. sit behind
  • The kick — practice final 100m surge in an 800m
  • Practice 800m with a planned move at 600m mark
  • Heat simulation (10 min):
  • Simulated 100m heats — react to gun, race others in the heat
  • Discuss what went well and adjustments for next time

Cool-Down (10 min)

  • 1 lap easy jog
  • Static stretches: full body
  • Mental preparation talk — visualization, staying calm before races
