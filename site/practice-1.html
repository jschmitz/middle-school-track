Practice 1

Welcome & Baseline

Warm-Up (10 min)

  • Team introductions — names, grades, events of interest
  • Review team rules and expectations
  • 2 laps easy jog (800m)
  • Dynamic stretches: leg swings, high knees, butt kicks, arm circles

Main Workout (40 min)

  • Baseline 100m time trial — two attempts, record best time
  • Baseline 400m time trial — one attempt at comfortable-hard effort
  • Baseline 800m time trial — focus on completing the distance
  • Standing long jump — three attempts, measure best
  • Record all results for tracking progress throughout the season

Cool-Down (10 min)

  • 1 lap easy walk/jog
  • Static stretches: quads, hamstrings, calves, hip flexors
  • Team huddle — preview the season, set goals
