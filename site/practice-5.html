Practice 5

Relay Basics

Warm-Up (10 min)

  • 2 laps easy jog (800m)
  • Dynamic stretches: A-skips, high knees, butt kicks, lateral shuffles
  • 2 x 80m build-ups

Main Workout (40 min)

  • Baton basics — proper grip, receiving hand position (palm back, thumb down)
  • Stationary handoff drill — pairs practice "hand" command and exchange
  • Walking handoffs — pairs walk through exchange zone, practice timing
  • Jogging handoffs — increase speed, focus on smooth exchange
  • Exchange zone rules — mark 20m zone, practice entering and exiting legally
  • Full-speed relay practice — 4 x 100m relay in teams of 4
  • Relay race — competitive 4 x 100m between teams

Cool-Down (10 min)

  • 1 lap easy jog
  • Static stretches: full body focus
  • Discuss common relay mistakes: early/late exchanges, leaving the zone
