Divisions

4th Grade

Competes alongside JV athletes but is scored separately. May run up to JV.

JV — 7th Grade & Under

JV athletes may run up to Varsity. Cannot compete in both JV and Varsity in the same event.

Varsity — 8th Grade

Varsity athletes cannot compete in the JV division.

All races have unlimited entries except the 100m Dash, 200m Dash (5 per division per gender per school), and relay maximums.
Awards (medals) go to 1st–3rd place in each division. Only the top 2 individual finishers and “A” relay teams from each school are eligible for scoring.
Scoring: Individual events — 10-8-6-5-4-3-2-1. Relays — 10-8-6-5-4-3-2-1.

Day-of Information for Parents

Parking

Parking is available on site. Allow extra time — the lot fills quickly before the meet.

No Pets

Pets are NOT allowed on campus.

Field Access

Spectators and non-competing athletes must stay off the infield. Only coaches are permitted on the infield. Stay clear of the finish line area.

Missed Events

If your athlete misses their event, they are scratched with no recourse. Make sure they are listening for their name and head to the check-in area immediately when their event is called.

Check-In / Clerking

Running event athletes check in at the clerking area by the 100m start line once their event is called. Field event athletes check in at the event site.
