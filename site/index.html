Practice Structure

Every practice follows the same framework so athletes know what to expect and build good habits.

1
Light Jog

Easy movement to elevate heart rate and loosen up

2
Dynamic Warm-Up

Leg swings, high knees, A-skips — preparing joints and muscles for intensity

3
Speed Work

Acceleration drills and strides to develop fast-twitch mechanics

4
Sprints

Short, maximal efforts to build top-end speed

5
Power

Bounds, jumps, and plyometrics to develop explosive strength

6
Targeted Learning

Form focus, event-specific skills, or technique breakdowns

7
Cool Down

Easy jog and static stretching to recover and stay injury-free
